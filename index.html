Welcome to the Waterloo Start Portal!

As a first year student you will experience a lot of change!
We are here to guide you through your new journey step by step.

Connect

This section will guide you through all the platforms Waterloo offers to connect with your peers, professors, and personal fincances.

Be Active!

As a new student you might find yourself very busy with your studies but it is important to maintain a healthy life style! Check out what Waterloo offers for your physical wellbeing.

Take Notice

Be curious, remark on the unusual, notice the changing seasons, savour the moment! This section will help you become familiar with Waterloo's campus map through a series of exploration quests.

Keep Learning

Waterloo offers many programs for students to become successful. Try something new and discover the resources available to you that will help you grow throughout your studies at UW.

Give

Do something nice for a friend, or a stranger! Joing a club or student society. Create positive connections with the people around you.

Your Profile

This section keeps track of what you have accomplished including your points you have earned. These points can be turned into Flex Dollars!
